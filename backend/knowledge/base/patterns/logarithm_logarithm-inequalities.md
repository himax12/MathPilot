### Example 1 (Year: 2023)

**Question:**

<p>The number of integral solutions $$x$$ of $$\log _{\left(x+\frac{7}{2}\right)}\left(\frac{x-7}{2 x-3}\right)^{2} \geq 0$$ is :</p>

**Solution:**

$$\log _{\left(x+\frac{7}{2}\right)}\left(\frac{x-7}{2 x-3}\right)^{2} \geq 0$$
<br/><br/><b>Domain :</b>
<br/><br/>$$
\begin{aligned}
& x+\frac{7}{2}>0 \\\\
& x>\frac{-7}{2} \\\\
& x+\frac{7}{2} \neq 1 \\\\
& x \neq \frac{-5}{2} \\\\
& \frac{x-7}{2 x-3} \neq 0 \\\\
& x \neq 7 \\\\
& x \neq \frac{3}{2}
\end{aligned}
$$
<br/><br/>$$
\text { Taking intersection : } x \in\left(\frac{-7}{2}, \infty\right)-\left\{-\frac{5}{2}, \frac{3}{2}, 7\right\}
$$
<br/><br/>Now $\log _{\mathrm{a}} \mathrm{b} \geq 0$ if $\mathrm{a}>1$ and $\mathrm{b} \geq 1$
<br/><br/>$$
\begin{gathered}
\text { Or } \\\\
a \in(0,1) \text { and } b \in(0,1)
\end{gathered}
$$
<br/><br/><b>Case I :</b> $$
x+\frac{7}{2}>1 \text { and }\left(\frac{x-7}{2 x-3}\right)^2 \geq 1
$$
<br/><br/>$$ \therefore $$ $$
x > -\frac{5}{2}
$$
<br/><br/>and 
<br/><br/>$$
\begin{aligned}
& (2 x-3)^2-(x-7)^2 \leq 0 \\\\
& (2 x-3+x-7)(2 x-3-x+7) \leq 0 \\\\
& (3 x-10)(x+4) \leq 0
\end{aligned}
$$
<br/><br/>$$
x \in\left[-4, \frac{10}{3}\right]...
