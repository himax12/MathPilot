## Worked Examples

### Example 1 (Year: 2023)

**Question:**

<p>$$\lim _\limits{n \rightarrow \infty}\left\{\left(2^{\frac{1}{2}}-2^{\frac{1}{3}}\right)\left(2^{\frac{1}{2}}-2^{\frac{1}{5}}\right) \ldots . .\left(2^{\frac{1}{2}}-2^{\frac{1}{2 n+1}}\right)\right\}$$ is equal to :</p>

**Solution:**

Let $L=\lim\limits_{n \rightarrow \infty}\left\{\left(2^{\frac{1}{2}}-2^{\frac{1}{3}}\right)\left(2^{\frac{1}{2}}-2^{\frac{1}{5}}\right) \ldots\left(2^{\frac{1}{2}}-2^{\frac{1}{2 n-1}}\right)\right\}$ 
<br/><br/>Here, $\left(2^{\frac{1}{2}}-2^{\frac{1}{3}}\right)^n<\left(2^{\frac{1}{2}}-2^{\frac{1}{3}}\right)\left(2^{\frac{1}{3}}-2^{\frac{1}{5}}\right)$
$$
\ldots\left(2^{\frac{1}{2}}-2^{\frac{1}{2 n-1}}\right)<\left(2^{\frac{1}{2}}-2^{\frac{1}{2 n-1}}\right)^n
$$
<br/><br/>$$
\Rightarrow \lim\limits_{n \rightarrow \infty}\left(2^{\frac{1}{2}}-2^{\frac{1}{3}}\right)^n < L < \lim\limits_{n \rightarrow \infty}\left(2^{\frac{1}{2}}-2^{\frac{1}{2 n-1}}\right)^n
$$

<br/><br/>Since, $\lim\limits_{n \rightarrow \infty}\left(2^{\frac{1}{2}}-2^{\frac{1}{3}}\right)^n=0$ and $\lim\limits_{n \rightarrow \infty}\left(2^{\frac{1}{2}}-2^{\frac{1}{2 n-1}}\right)^n=0$
<br/><br/>$$\therefore L = 0$$
