### Example 4 (Year: 2024)

**Question:**

<p>$$\text { Number of integral terms in the expansion of }\left\{7^{\left(\frac{1}{2}\right)}+11^{\left(\frac{1}{6}\right)}\right\}^{824} \text { is equal to _________. }$$</p>

**Solution:**

<p>General term in expansion of $$\left((7)^{1 / 2}+(11)^{1 / 6}\right)^{824}$$ is $$\mathrm{t}_{\mathrm{r}+1}={ }^{824} \mathrm{C}_{\mathrm{r}}(7)^{\frac{824-\mathrm{r}}{2}}(11)^{\mathrm{r} / 6}$$</p>
<p>For integral term, $$r$$ must be multiple of 6.</p>
<p>Hence $$r=0,6,12, ....... 822$$</p>
