### Example 1 (Year: 2002)

**Question:**

In a triangle with sides $$a, b, c,$$ $${r_1} &gt; {r_2} &gt; {r_3}$$ (which are the ex-radii) then  :

**Solution:**

$${r_1} &gt; {r_2} &gt; {r_3}$$
<br><br>$$ \Rightarrow {\Delta  \over {s - a}} &gt; {\Delta  \over {s - b}} &gt; {\Delta  \over {s - c}};$$
<br><br>$$ \Rightarrow s - a &lt; s - b &lt; s - c$$
<br><br>$$ \Rightarrow  - a &lt;  - b &lt;  - c$$
<br><br>$$ \Rightarrow a &gt; b &gt; c$$
